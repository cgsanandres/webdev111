OUR STORY

Born from a passion for esports and community, Respawn Gaming Hub was built to give Filipino gamers a world-class space to play, compete, and grow together.

FROM A DREAM TO A REALITY

Founded in 2023 by a group of competitive gamers who struggled to find proper facilities for training and tournaments, Respawn started as a small 10-station setup in a Quezon City garage.

Within just two years, we expanded to a 50+ station hub with console corners, VR booths, and a dedicated tournament stage – all designed to deliver peak performance and an unforgettable gaming experience.

Our mission is simple: to elevate Philippine esports by providing top-tier equipment, fostering a supportive community, and hosting events that give local talent a chance to shine on the big stage.

WHAT MAKES US STAND OUT

TOP-TIER EQUIPMENT

Every rig is built with latest-gen components to ensure zero lag and maximum performance for competitive play.

SUPPORTIVE COMMUNITY

From casual players to pro teams, we foster a welcoming space where everyone can learn, collaborate, and level up.

REGULAR EVENTS

Monthly tournaments, LAN parties, and cosplay nights keep the energy high and give gamers a chance to compete for prizes.
