Accessibility & House Rules

Accessibility Features

  • Slip-resistant ramp access to all floors
  • Wheelchair-friendly restrooms (2 per floor)
  • Designated priority seating areas
  • On-site assistance staff available
  • Braille signage in key corridors
  • Hearing loop system in main hall

House Rules & Guidelines

  • No outside food/drinks except sealed water bottles
  • Professional cameras allowed (no detachable lenses over 50mm without permit)
  • Smoking permitted only in designated outdoor patio areas
  • No weapons, illegal substances, or hazardous items on premises
  • Re-entry allowed with valid wristband or stamped ticket
  • Pets not permitted except certified service animals
  • Audio/video recording prohibited unless authorized by event organizers
