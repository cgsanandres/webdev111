Frequently Asked Questions
Account & Login
How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link will be valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact support.
Why can't I log into my account?
Common reasons include entering an incorrect username or password, your account being temporarily locked after multiple failed attempts, or a browser issue. Try resetting your password first. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or contact support to verify your account status.
How do I update my personal information?
Log into your account, go to the "Patient Profile" page, and click "Edit Profile". You can update most personal information there. For sensitive changes like your PhilSys ID or date of birth, you'll need to submit a request through the support form and provide valid identification documents.
Appointments
How do I book or reschedule an appointment?
Go to the "Appointments" page and click "Book New Appointment" to schedule a visit. To reschedule an existing appointment, select it from your upcoming appointments list and choose "Reschedule". You can also cancel appointments at least 24 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees.
What should I bring to my in-person appointment?
Please bring a valid government-issued ID, your insurance card (if applicable), any prescribed medications you're currently taking, previous medical records or lab results relevant to your visit, and a list of any symptoms or questions you have for your doctor.
How do I join a video consultation?
A video consultation link will be sent to your registered email and mobile number 30 minutes before your appointment time. You can also find the link in the "Upcoming Appointments" section of your dashboard. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and enable camera/microphone access in your browser or mobile app.
Billing & Insurance
How do I view and pay my bills online?
Go to the "Insurance & Billing" page to view all outstanding and paid bills. Select a bill to view details and choose from available payment options including credit/debit card, GCash, PayMaya, or bank transfer. Payment confirmations are sent immediately via email and SMS.
Which insurance providers do you accept?
We accept all major HMO providers including Maxicare, Medicard, PhilHealth, Cocolife, Intellicare, and Fortune Care. We also work with international insurance providers for eligible patients. Please check with your provider or our support team to confirm coverage for specific services.
How do I submit a claim for reimbursement?
Download the reimbursement form from the "Insurance & Billing" page, fill it out completely, and attach all required documents including official receipts, itemized statements, and your insurance provider's claim form. Submit everything via email to billing@healthsync.ph or in person at our support center.
Medical Records & Prescriptions
How do I access my medical records and lab results?
Log into your account and go to the "Health Records" page to view all available records, lab results, and doctor's notes. Most results are posted within 24-48 hours after testing. You can download or print copies for your personal records or to share with other healthcare providers.
How do I request a prescription refill?
Go to the "Prescriptions" page, select the medication you need refilled, and click "Request Refill". Refill requests are reviewed by your prescribing doctor within 24 hours. Once approved, you can choose to pick up your medication at our clinic or have it delivered to your address for a small fee.
