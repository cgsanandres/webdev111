The Legacy of Crystal Bay

A Sanctuary of Serenity

Founded on the principle of harmonious luxury, Luna Vista Resort has been a cornerstone of coastal elegance for over two decades. What began as a private family estate has transformed into a globally recognized sanctuary for those seeking the ultimate tropical escape.

Our philosophy is simple: to merge world-class hospitality with the raw, unspoiled beauty of the ocean. Every suite is designed to capture the gentle rhythms of the tide, and every meal is a celebration of local artisanal flavors.

Our Commitment

Ecological Elegance

At Luna Vista, we believe true luxury is sustainable. Our resort operates with a zero-plastic initiative and supports the Crystal Bay Coral Restoration project. We invite our guests to experience a paradise that we are actively preserving for the generations to follow.
