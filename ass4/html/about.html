Our Story

Paws Haven began with a small group of volunteers who believed that every animal deserves love, safety, and a place to call home. What started as a community effort to rescue stray animals quickly grew into a dedicated shelter focused on rehabilitation and responsible adoption.

We provide a sanctuary where abandoned pets receive premium medical care and the emotional support they need to heal before meeting their forever families.

Our mission is simple: Rescue, Care, and Rehome.
