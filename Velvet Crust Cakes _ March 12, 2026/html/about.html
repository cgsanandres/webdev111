The 72-Hour Promise

At Velvet Crust, we believe that true luxury cannot be rushed. Every cake that leaves our kitchen is the result of a three-day ritual: from the slow-tempering of our signature cocoa to the precision of our hand-piped finishes.

We don't just bake; we curate an experience of flavor and structural integrity that honors the heritage of classic pâtisserie while embracing modern minimalism.
